# Criticism and Self-Criticism — Original Textual Basis

### "On Coalition Government"《论联合政府》(April 1945)

**One of the three great styles of work:**
> "Armed with Marxist-Leninist theory and ideology, the Communist Party of China has brought a new style of work to the Chinese people, a style of work that essentially entails integrating theory with practice, forging close links with the masses, and practicing self-criticism."

**The presence or absence of self-criticism as a distinguishing marker:**
> "Conscientious practice of self-criticism is another hallmark distinguishing our Party from all other political parties. As we have said, a room must be regularly cleaned, or dust will accumulate; a face must be regularly washed, or it too will be covered with dust."

### "On Correcting Mistaken Ideas in the Party"《关于纠正党内的错误思想》(December 1929)

> "Inner-Party criticism is a weapon for strengthening the Party organization and increasing its fighting capacity."

### "Rectify the Party's Style of Work"《整顿党的作风》(February 1942)

**Learn from past mistakes to avoid future ones; treat the illness to save the patient:**
> "Past errors must be exposed with no regard for personal feelings; they must be analyzed and criticized with a scientific attitude so that future work may be done more carefully and better. This is the meaning of 'learn from past mistakes to avoid future ones.' But our purpose in exposing errors and criticizing shortcomings is the same as a doctor's in treating a disease — entirely to save the patient, not to put the patient to death."
> "Anyone who has committed errors, as long as they do not conceal their ailment or persist in their mistakes to the point of becoming incurable, but instead are honest and genuinely willing to be treated, willing to make corrections — we should welcome them, cure their ailment, and help them become good comrades."

### "Serve the People"《为人民服务》(September 1944)

> "Because we serve the people, if we have shortcomings, we are not afraid to have them pointed out and criticized. It does not matter who points them out to us. As long as you are right, we will correct ourselves. If your suggestions are good for the people, we will act accordingly."
