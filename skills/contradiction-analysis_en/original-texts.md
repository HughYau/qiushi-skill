# Contradiction Analysis — Original Textual Basis

### "On Contradiction"《矛盾论》(August 1937)

**The universality of contradiction:**
> "The universality or absoluteness of contradiction has a twofold meaning. One is that contradiction exists in the process of development of all things, and the other is that in the process of development of each thing, a movement of opposites exists from beginning to end."

**The particularity of contradiction:**
> "Not only must we study the particular contradiction and the essence defined by it in every great system of the forms of material motion, but we must also study the particular contradiction and the essence of each process in the long course of development of each form of material motion."
> "Making a concrete analysis of concrete conditions" — this is "the most essential thing in Marxism, the living soul of Marxism."

**The principal contradiction and the principal aspect of a contradiction:**
> "In the process of development of a complex thing, there are many contradictions in existence, and among them there must be one that is the principal contradiction whose existence and development determines or influences the existence and development of the other contradictions."
> "The principal and the non-principal aspects of a contradiction transform into each other, and the nature of a thing changes accordingly."

**The identity and struggle of the aspects of a contradiction:**
> "All contradictory things are interconnected; not only do they coexist in a single entity under certain conditions, but in other certain conditions they also transform into each other. This is the full meaning of the identity of contradictions."

### "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People"《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》(February 1957)

**Two types of contradictions differing in nature:**
> "There are two types of social contradictions facing us — contradictions between ourselves and the enemy, and contradictions among the people. These are two types of contradictions that are completely different in nature."
> "Contradictions between ourselves and the enemy are antagonistic contradictions. Contradictions among the people, among the working people, are non-antagonistic."

**Different methods of resolution for different natures:**
> "The method of dictatorship is used [for enemy contradictions]... the method of democracy, of persuasion and education, is used to resolve contradictions among the people."
> The formula of "unity — criticism — unity": "Starting from the desire for unity, resolving contradictions through criticism or struggle, and thereby achieving a new unity on a new basis."
