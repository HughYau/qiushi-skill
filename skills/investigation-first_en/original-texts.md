# Investigation First — Original Textual Basis

### "Oppose Book Worship"《反对本本主义》(May 1930)

**The necessity of investigation:**
> "If you have not investigated a particular problem, you are deprived of the right to speak on it. Is that not too brutal? Not in the least. Since you have not investigated the current and historical conditions of that problem and know nothing of its essentials, whatever you say about it is bound to be nonsense. Nonsense, as everyone knows, cannot solve problems — so what is unjust about depriving you of your right to speak?"

**Investigation means solving the problem:**
> "Investigation may be likened to 'ten months of pregnancy,' and solving a problem to 'the single day of birth.' Investigation is solving the problem."

**Opposing book worship:**
> "We say Marxism is correct not because Marx was some kind of 'prophet,' but because his theory has been proven correct in our practice and in our struggles." "The Marxism we speak of is Marxism that is alive and plays an effective role in the life and struggles of the masses, not Marxism that is confined to mere lip service."

**Methods of investigation:**
> "Holding investigation meetings is the simplest, most feasible, and most reliable method. I have benefited greatly from this method; it is a school more advanced than any university."
> "Those attending the meeting... should be people with a thorough understanding of socioeconomic conditions. In terms of age, older people are best... but young people should also be included... In terms of occupation... In terms of the number of participants, at least three people are needed... In terms of preparation for the investigation... One should serve as the chairperson and personally pose the questions... The investigation should take the form of discussions... One must go in-depth... and one must keep the records personally."

### "Reform Our Study"《改造我们的学习》(May 1941)

> "We should proceed from objective, actually existing things, derive laws from them, and use these as a guide for our action. For this purpose, as Marx said, one must take possession of material in detail and subject it to scientific analysis and synthesis."

**Three deficiencies that need correcting:**
> "The first type: not paying attention to studying current conditions." "The second type: not paying attention to studying history." "The third type: when studying Marxism-Leninism (general principles), studying theory for theory's sake rather than seeking from it a standpoint, a viewpoint, and a method for solving the theoretical and strategic problems of the Chinese revolution."
