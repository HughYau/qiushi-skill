# Overall Planning — Original Textual Basis

### "On the Ten Major Relationships"《论十大关系》(April 1956)

**The general policy:**
> "These ten questions are all raised around one basic policy: to mobilize all positive factors, domestic and international, to serve the cause of socialism."

**Opposing one-sidedness:**
> Mao Zedong analyzed ten pairs of relationships one by one (heavy industry vs. light industry and agriculture, coastal industry vs. inland industry, economic development vs. national defense, the state vs. the individual, the central government vs. local authorities, etc.). The core method was: acknowledging the validity of both sides in each pair of relationships, and not one-sidedly emphasizing one while neglecting the other.

**Learning from others' lessons:**
> "Recently the Soviet Union has exposed some shortcomings and errors in their process of building socialism. Do you still want to follow the same detours they took? In the past, it was precisely because we drew on their experience and lessons that we were able to avoid some detours; now, of course, we should take this all the more as a warning."

### "On the Correct Handling of Contradictions Among the People"《关于正确处理人民内部矛盾的问题》(February 1957)

**Overall planning and making appropriate arrangements:**
> "Our policy is to practice overall planning with all-round consideration and make appropriate arrangements. Whether it is the problem of grain, natural disasters, employment, education, intellectuals, the united front of all patriotic forces, ethnic minorities, or any other issue — all must proceed from the standpoint of overall planning for the entire people, and appropriate arrangements should be made through consultation with various parties according to the actual conditions possible at the time and place."

### "On Contradiction"《矛盾论》(August 1937)

> "Not only must we study the particular contradiction and the essence defined by it in every great system of the forms of material motion, but we must also study the particular contradiction and the essence of each process in the long course of development of each form of material motion."

— The prerequisite for overall planning is a thorough understanding of the particularity of every pair of contradictions within the system.
